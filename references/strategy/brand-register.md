# Brand Register

Choose the brand route before visual style. This is the webpage equivalent of subject routing in `master-styles`.

## Registers

### Planetary Authority

For: globes, maps, tourism, logistics, cultural export, global education, geopolitics, planetary data.

- hero: rotating globe / orbital atlas / world-to-product reveal
- material: celestial black box, glass, brass, illuminated map paper
- motion: slow rotation, orbital snap, atmospheric reveal
- proof: regions, routes, export points, cultural destinations
- linked skills: `master-styles`, `article-visual-grammar`, Image Gen

### Material Desire

For: cosmetics, textile, furniture, fashion, food, jewelry, craft.

- hero: object/material macro stage
- material: glass, serum, fabric, wood, paper, ceramic, metal
- motion: light sweep, fold, thread pull, droplet gather, surface cutaway
- proof: ingredient, craft step, material origin, product detail
- linked skills: `master-styles`, `cover-pop`, Image Gen

### Mechanism Prestige

For: SaaS, AI systems, education platforms, consulting methods, industrial capabilities.

- hero: layered artifact / cutaway / capability engine
- material: glass instrument, technical plate, editorial paper model
- motion: split, inspect, pass-through, assemble
- proof: workflow states, screenshots, method steps, outcomes
- linked skills: `article-visual-grammar`, `paper-operators` when physical action clarifies

### Human Credibility

For: consultants, founders, teachers, speakers, teams, personal brands.

- hero: portrait identity field
- material: portrait particles, evidence orbits, editorial lighting
- motion: gather, focus lens, proof lock-in
- proof: cases, industries, credentials, frameworks
- linked skills: `master-styles`, `article-visual-grammar`, Image Gen

### Cultural Worldbuilding

For: heritage, festival, tourism, exhibitions, culture companies, creative agencies.

- hero: immersive archive/night route/stage
- material: lantern, archive paper, map, landmark, ritual, stage light
- motion: route lights, archive unfolds, gallery pan
- proof: place, history, craft, event, cultural export
- linked skills: `paper-operators`, `master-styles`, `cover-pop`, Image Gen

### Evidence Authority

For: reports, policy, finance, research, technical whitepapers.

- hero: evidence constellation or field atlas
- material: documents, charts, cards, stamps, proof nodes
- motion: zoom into evidence, filter, classify, resolve
- proof: exact claims, numbers, sources
- linked skills: `article-visual-grammar`, `master-styles`

## Example: 地球仪产品

```text
brand route: Planetary Authority + Material Desire
source assets: product globe photo, map texture, product specs, market/use cases
hero: cosmic Earth rotates in darkness; scroll reveals meridian ring, stand, material; Earth resolves into the physical globe product.
motion signature: orbit -> alignment -> product reveal.
proof chapters:
- material/craft closeup
- educational/global route use case
- collection/display/luxury home scene
- final product still with CTA
linked skills:
- master-styles for brass/map/luxury restraint tokens
- article-visual-grammar for source anchors and chapter proof
- Image Gen if product stage or environment is missing
reject if:
- globe is just a generic earth
- product image is not visible by hero resolution
- orbit labels are fake
```
