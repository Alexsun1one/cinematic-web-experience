Not every brief wants a spinning object.

For regulated, consulting, education, finance, medical, or enterprise proof work, the premium route is often calm evidence: claim, proof, risk, assumption, and source anchors in one inspectable theater.

Claim to proof path

This route makes the source document navigable. It does not invent metrics or animate claims beyond the evidence supplied.

ProofWhich source fragment, number, case, or quote supports it?
RiskWhich assumption must stay labeled to avoid false certainty?

Prototype truth: this example uses representative proposal anchors. In a real build, every node would link to a source page, quote, metric, screenshot, or declared assumption. The calm trust route intentionally avoids unnecessary 3D spectacle.
